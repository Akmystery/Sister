About Me

Hello I am Deborah Bellamy. Let me introduce myself. I am fulfilled as a Communication Consultant, Coach, Trainer. I have been running my own business for over the last 20 years and have freelanced most of my working life. I am based in Vienna, Austria and work in Austria, Eastern Europe and India.I have trained, coached, and consulted, using both conventional and less conventional approaches. My background as a Superlearning teacher in Adult Education, G.I.M (Guided Imagery and Music for individuals / groups, and a presentation trainer have influnenced me as a Certified Nonviolent Communication Trainer (CNVC - certified since 2003) for organisations, institutions, couples and individuals.
